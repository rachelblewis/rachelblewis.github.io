BBQ Chicken Pizza

Ingredients

1 Chicken Breast
BBQ Sauce
Olive Oil
Pizza Dough from Trader Joes
Mozzarella Cheese
Optional --> Minced Garlic

Tools

Rolling Pin

Pizza Cutter

Pizza Pan

Bowl

Directions

1. Preheat over to 350 degrees (assuming not doing dough work explained below)

2. Brush a bowl with olive oil, put dough in and let sit covered with plastic for 30-40 minutes if possible (I skip this step sometimes)

3. Roll out dough to 10-12 inches.. check package. Cover again and let rise for another half hour

4. Wash and dry the chicken, put on salt, pepper, and bbq on each side and bake at 350 for 25 ish minutes depending on thickness of the breast, could be a little more or a little less. Let cool, then cut/rip into 1/2 inch cubes/pieces

5. Oil the pizza sheet, move dough to the baking sheet, put a tiny bit of minced garlic on the dough, then cover with bbq sauce, cheese, and chicken

6. Bake at 350 for 12ish minutes (package says 10-12) but we always think it takes a bit longer.. bake until cheese bubbles
